How to play

There are three parts to the game:

Word Game

Randomly generated letters will appear on screen. Players have 30 seconds to determine the longest word. Each of the letters can be used only once. Player 1 must input their word first and then player 2. The longest word achieves the highes score!

Numbers Game

Randomly generated numbers will appear on screen. Players have 30 seconds to accumulate the target number. Each of the numbers can be used only once. When the time is up the players must input their number using +, -, *, / and as many brackets as needed. The closest to the target wins!

Countdown Conundrum

A nine letter word will be scrambled on screen. Determine the word and win the points!!

Good Luck!!
